Top 5 for Nerds

The nerdiest and most controversial rankings. Ready to debate?

🎬 Top 5 Movies for Nerds according to CodeFlix:

  1. The Matrix (1999) – For the code and tech philosophy
  2. Iron Man (2008) – Perfect fusion of programming and action
  3. Ready Player One (2018) – Gaming culture and virtual reality
  4. Tron: Legacy (2010) – A journey inside an operating system
  5. Ex Machina (2015) – AI, ethics, and thought engineering

🎮 Top 5 Video Games for Nerds according to CodeFlix:

  1. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild (2017) – Open world exploration and adventure
  2. Portal 2 (2011) – Mind-bending puzzles and dark humor
  3. Half-Life 2 (2004) – Groundbreaking physics and storytelling
  4. Mass Effect 2 (2010) – Epic sci-fi narrative and character development
  5. The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt (2015) – Immersive world and rich storytelling
